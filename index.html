Giving Back

InstaMom University Gives Back

We have devoted our lives to education, and we strongly believe in—and have witnessed—its transformative power. We also know that it is incredibly expensive. So we put our money where our values are: for every 1000 boxes sold, we will randomly select the name of one customer (one entry per purchase) to receive a $1000 scholarship from InstaMom U as a token of our appreciation to our customers, and our support for students.

About Us

Hi, and welcome to InstaMom U! We are Dr. Beth Savage and Dr. Virginia Cylke, former professors with a combined 55 years of experience teaching and mentoring college students. Though we have left the classroom, we want to continue to support students and help them to be confident and successful. So we created the InstaMom model of care packages that nurture the whole person. We hope you find something truly special here for your student!
